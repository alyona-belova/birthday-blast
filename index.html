HOW TO PLAY:

  • Use A / D or Left / Right Arrow keys to move your spaceship
  • Press SPACEBAR to shoot candies at the alien invaders
  • Protect the birthday cake at the bottom of the screen from alien attacks
  • Defeat all aliens in a wave to progress to the next wave
  • Clear 3 waves to reveal the special birthday message!
  • If the cake's health reaches 0%, the game is over
